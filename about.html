    Hi, I'm Olivia

    A First Class Fashion Design graduate from Nottingham Trent University and a multidisciplinary designer who loves turning ideas into something people can see, experience and connect with.

    My degree gave me a strong foundation in design, but my creativity has never fitted into just one area. I enjoy graphic design, branding, illustration, creative direction and developing visual concepts. I am naturally curious and love learning new skills, especially when a project gives me the chance to try something different.

    What I enjoy most is starting with a small idea and seeing where it can go. I like researching, sketching, experimenting and working things out as I go. Sometimes the final result is completely different from where I started, and that is often the most exciting part.

    I'm drawn to creative work that has personality, tells a story and makes people feel something. Outside of designing, I'm usually working through my bucket list, finding inspiration in unexpected places or spending time in nature with my dogs.
